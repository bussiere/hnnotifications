HN Notifications
About | Settings

Get an email as soon as a Hacker News story reaches your custom score threshold.

No need to create an account, nor to provide any password. Just enter your email address, and activate your subscription through the verification link we'll send to your inbox.

HN Notifications is an open source project, written in Go. Please, feel free to contribute!

HN Notifications is in no way affiliated with Y Combinator.
